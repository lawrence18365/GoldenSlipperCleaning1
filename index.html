About Golden Slipper

Welcome to Golden Slipper Cleaning Services, a luxury cleaning business born out of passion and perseverance. Founded by Abbie, our journey began three years ago with a vision to create a cleaning service that truly makes a difference in people's lives.

After overcoming personal challenges and embracing a holistic lifestyle, Abbie discovered her passion for low-toxic essential oils. This newfound purpose led to the creation of Golden Slipper Cleaning Services, where we combine professional expertise with holistic practices.

Our mission is to provide high-detailed, bespoke cleaning services infused with essential oils, aromatherapy, and organic products. We're dedicated to creating not just clean spaces, but harmonious environments that promote wellbeing.
