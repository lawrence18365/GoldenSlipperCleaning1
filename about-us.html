Welcome to Golden Slipper

At Golden Slipper Cleaning Services, our story began in 2021 with a vision to make a difference in people's lives. Our founder, Abbie, embarked on a journey fueled by perseverance and dedication. Overcoming personal health challenges really sparked a passion for non-toxic cleaning products and a more holistic lifestyle.

Through self-education, Abbie discovered the incredible benefits of combining non-toxic products with essential oils, leading to amazing results. Today, Golden Slipper Cleaning Services offers high-attention-to-detail cleaning that stands out from the rest.

We save you precious time, allowing you to focus on what matters most - yourself, your family, and your business. We understand that every client is unique, which is why we offer bespoke cleaning services tailored to your specific needs. Our packages are curated to meet your individual requirements, ensuring a personalized cleaning experience.

Our Mission

At Golden Slipper Cleaning Services, our mission is to provide exceptional, eco-friendly cleaning solutions that promote a healthier living environment and enhance the overall quality of life for our clients. We are committed to:

  • Utilizing non-toxic, environmentally friendly cleaning products
  • Delivering personalized, high-quality cleaning services
  • Promoting wellness through cleaner, safer living spaces
  • Educating our clients about the benefits of non-toxic cleaning
  • Continuously improving our methods and staying updated with the latest eco-friendly cleaning technologies
